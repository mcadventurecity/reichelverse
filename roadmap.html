Roadmap

The Reichelverse will spread over multiple phases of storytelling, similar to the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Phase 1: September 2022 - August 2024

The first phase serves to introduce the setting of the Reichelverse as well as the initial cast of core characters. It will consist of the first four arcs of the Campbell Chronicles serial, as well as various short stories, and at least three novels.

  • The Campbell Chronicles Act I: Freshman Year - begins September 9, 2022 - Set 2041-2042
  • The Campbell Chronicles Act II: Sophomore Year - begins February 2023 - Set 2042-2043
  • The Siepler Dynasty Book One: The Move - Releases June 2023 - Set 2015
  • The Campbell Chronicles Act III: Junior Year - begins August 2023 - Set 2043-2044
  • Rise to Riches: A Reichelverse Novel - Releases November 2023 - Set 2023-2041
  • The Campbell Chronicles Act IV: Senior Year - Begins January 2024 - Set 2044-2045
  • TBD Crossover Novel - Releases August 2024 - Set Summer 2045

The Siepler Dynasty Book One: The Move:
When Bill Siepler, the Mayor of a remote, small fishing village in Alaska, receives an offer to work for a federal government office in Washington, DC, it feels like his many years of moving all over the nation are at an end. After finding a new home in a quickly-growing town on the outskirts of the nation's capital, Bill decides he is going to turn this move into the biggest family road trip of his life. However, living in one of America's most remote regions means this will be difficult. Along with his wife Michelle, his Son Josh, and daughter Rosalynn, Bill embarks on the biggest, most chaotic, and fun road trip of all time.
